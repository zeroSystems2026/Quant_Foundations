# CS50 Week 00: Scratch

* Binary Logic: The limit of a computer's function is determined by 1s (on) and 0s (off) based on their location in a given number of bits, ranging from 8-, 16-, 32-, and 64-bits.
* ASCII: An 8-bit legend of 1s and 0s that translates to English letters and symbols, such as "A" and "!".
* Colors: Colors are shown by 3-byte mixtures of red, green, and blue that correspond, within an image, to different combinations of 1s and 0s.
