# Case Study: Troubleshooting Fractional Crystallization Yields under Time Constraints

* **Situation:** During a General Chemistry lab, my partner and I were tasked with performing a Fractional Crystallization and Solubility Analysis to isolate and purify a specific amount of required solute from an impure mixture.
* **Task:** Our initial attempts resulted in amounts below the required yield threshold. As time progressed, I was faced with the challenge of diagnosing procedural errors and working under a time constraint as other teams finished their work.
