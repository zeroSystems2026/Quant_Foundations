# Case Study: Troubleshooting Recrystallization Yields under Time Constraints

* **Situation:** During a General Chemistry lab, I was tasked with using the recrystallization method to isolate and purify a specific amount of required solute.
* **Task:** Our initial attempts resulted in amounts well below the required yield threshold. As time progressed, I was faced with the challenge of diagnosing procedural errors and working under a time constraint as other teams finished their work.
